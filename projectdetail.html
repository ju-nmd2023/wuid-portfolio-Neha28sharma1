Coffee table book details

These are the two spreads from a coffe table book. The spreads are about a famous graphic designer "Stefan Sagmeister". He is known for his typography skills.Stefan Sagmeister is one of the most imaginative and original creative thinkers around today. Mention his name to any graphic designer and chances are you will get enthusiastic words of admiration and respect and with good reason! This award-winning designer has created some of the boldest, most innovative, most engaging work around, especially his type and handwriting-inspired solutions. Sagmeister makes type come alive sometimes almost literally. He takes type and fashions solutions that are visually dynamic expressions, above and beyond the actual meaning of the words. He will on occasion use a human canvas whether on himself or others with striking results.
